Where It All Began



To be honest, computing wasn’t my first passion in life. When I was younger, football was what I cared about most, and I spent the majority of my time playing the sport and enjoying every moment on the field. However, after going through a number of injuries, I gradually turned my attention to other areas. That was when I started to develop an interest in technology and computing, which slowly grew into an important part of my life alongside football.

Below is the overview of my experience in computing throughout the years:


Year Achievements
2013 Learning how to use computer for the first time (I was only 6 years old at that time)
2018 Learning to create basic programs using Scratch
2020 Getting the first MOS certificate for Microsoft Excel
2022 Learning to program using Python
2025 Learning to program using Java


I Nearly Gave Up on Computing



During my journey of learning how to program, I faced many challenges, with some problems taking me days to solve. There were times when I felt I wasn’t good enough to learn programming. However, my curiosity and determination kept me moving forward, and that persistence is the reason I am still learning and improving my coding skills today.

P.S. Even though I started exploring computing a little later than many others, it has become the field that excites my curiosity the most, and I am eager to continue developing myself in this area.
